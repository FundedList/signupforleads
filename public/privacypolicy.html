At Fundedlist LLC, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we use your mobile information. This page outlines our Privacy Policy and SMS/Text Messaging Terms & Conditions, in compliance with TCPA and CTIA requirements.

Privacy Policy

Information Collection

When you opt in to our SMS/text messaging program, we collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Your consent to receive text messages
  • Any information you provide in response to our messages
Information Use

We use your mobile information only for:

  • Delivering SMS/text messages you have opted in to receive
  • Customer service and support related to our services
  • Operational notifications related to your relationship with Fundedlist LLC
Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or rent mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers/Subcontractors: Trusted vendors providing services on our behalf (e.g., customer support, message delivery).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties for any other purpose.
Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

SMS Terms & Conditions

Program Description

By providing your mobile number and opting in, you consent to receive recurring SMS/text messages from Fundedlist LLC, including updates, alerts, and information related to our services. Message frequency may vary.

Opt-In and Consent

By opting in, you confirm you are the owner or authorized user of the mobile number provided. Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchase.

Opt-Out

You can cancel SMS/text messages at any time by replying STOP. After you send STOP, you will receive one confirmation message, and then you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

Help

If you need assistance, reply HELP to any of our messages or contact us at support@fundedlist.com.
